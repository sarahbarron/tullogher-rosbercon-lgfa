Abby makes the U14 County Team

Well done to Abby who has made the U14 county team.

Abby has worked very hard both at training and in her spare time to improve her Gaelic Football skills. She never misses a training session and having only started playing when our club started in 2016 all her hard work has paid off.

We are so proud to have one of our girls representing the club at county level and to have such a fantastic role model for the younger girls to aspire to.

Keep up the great work Abby.
