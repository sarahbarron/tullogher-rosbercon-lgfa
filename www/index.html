About Our Club

Tullogher Rosbercon ladies football club was founded in 2016. Since then we have won an U14 & an U12 league final. Many of our club girls also represented us in the primary school league. They won the county final in both 2016 and 2017.

We have teams from U8's right up to U16.

We are currently looking for girls of all ages to come and play with us regardless of if they have played before. >We also always welcome new members and volunteers to help with the running of the club.
